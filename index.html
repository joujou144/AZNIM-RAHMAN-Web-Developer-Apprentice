Hi, I'm Aznim. I'm a self-taught web developer with a keen eye for UI effects. I love CSS, my recently-developed super skill. I wake up everyday just to create something intuitive and dynamic with it. Expressive and resilient, I am a problem-solver who is always looking to create great user interfaces, writing solid code and takes pride in building best-in-class solutions.

Self-discipline and my abundant energy fuels me in the pursuit of cultivating skills in a complex discipline of programming whilst raising my child single-handedly has made a powerhouse multitasker who strives for nothing short of excellence in whatever challenge I may be presented with.
