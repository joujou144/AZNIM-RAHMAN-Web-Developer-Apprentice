Hi, I'm Aznim. I'm a self-taught web developer apprentice with an eye for UI effects and creating intuitive, dynamic user experiences. I am expressive, resilient, instinctual and an ultimate risk-taker who is always looking for best design practices.

My career-change journey from the medical device industry to web development began more out of necessity than raw ambition. As someone who deeply believes in herself and has a tremendous ability to cope with adversity, I have a knack for turning dead ends into open roads. Hard skills can be taught but personality is inherent.

As I collided dissimilar universes; sparking ideas and crossing off possibilities of what I wanted to do, finally I discovered something that was totally me. Something radically different - that requires mastery in design which helps convey my creativity skills whilst embracing the functionality of tech at the same time. I genuinely believe anything worth achieving is almost always difficult and for this reason, I find peace and comfort in being uncomfortable as it is the prime impetus as how I thrive and flourish.

My abundant energy fuels me in the pursuit of many interests, areas of study and artistic endeavors whilst raising my child single-handedly full time. I am an exceptionally fast learner and a powerhouse multitasker who strives for nothing short of excellence in whatever challenge I may be presented with. Currently I am seeking to launch a career as a hard-working asset to any team to learn, grow technical skills and develop long term.
